      How leverage EV works

      ▾

      Standard expected value multiplies win probability by points. But in a pool, a correct pick only matters if it separates you from the field. If 90% of your pool also picked Duke to the Final Four, being right gains you nothing.

      Leverage EV = Model Prob x ESPN Points x 1/(1 + Ownership x (Pool Size - 1))

      Ownership = % of public brackets with this pick
      The denominator = expected # of people sharing your pick
      Small pool: denominator stays small, chalk is fine
      Mega pool: denominator explodes, only unique picks have value

      This is why Iowa State at 2% champion ownership has higher leverage EV than Duke at 25% in a 500-person pool, even though Duke is more likely to win it all.
